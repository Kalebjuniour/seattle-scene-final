Travel Itinerary

  • Day 1

    • Visit the Space Needle in the morning
    • Explore the exhibits and attractions at the Museum of Pop Culture.

  • Day 2

    • Take a leisurely walk through the bustling aisles and corners of Pike Place Market, immersing yourself in its vibrant atmosphere.
    • Embark on a journey by taking a ride on a cruise that sails through the harbor.

  • Day 3

    • Take some time to unwind and rejuvenate at a beach park that is conveniently located nearby, and spend quality time with your family by enjoying a picnic.
