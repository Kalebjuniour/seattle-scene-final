Top 4 Food Spots in Seattle

The Walrus and the Carpenter

The Walrus and the Carpenter: Located in Ballard, this French restaurant is famous for its oysters and seafood dishes. It's highly recommended for its happy hour and vibrant atmosphere.

Pike Place Chowder

Seattle's iconic chowder and seafood destination with two locations at Pike Place Market and Pacific Place.

Intimate space for set-price dinners & drinks with Pacific Northwest-inspired Filipino flavors.
